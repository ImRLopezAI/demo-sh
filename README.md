
---
## 🔗 What is Uplink?

Uplink is a cloud-native platform designed to unify and streamline essential business functions through a modular suite of services

---

## ⚙️ Core Services

### 🛒 `uplink/market`  
Your e-commerce engine. Build, manage, and operate product catalogs, carts, and orders with APIs that support scalable online storefronts.

This module provides the following features:
- Product Catalog Management: Create, update, and organize products with support for variants, pricing, and inventory tracking.
- Cart and Checkout APIs: Seamlessly manage shopping carts, apply discounts, and process orders with secure payment integrations.
- Customer Management: Handle customer profiles, order history, and personalized recommendations.
- Scalability: Designed to support high traffic volumes and large product catalogs with efficient database queries and caching strategies.


### 📊 `uplink/insight`  
Analytics and inventory management in one. Monitor stock, detect trends, and optimize your resources using real-time business intelligence.

This module provides the following features:
- Inventory Tracking: Real-time monitoring of stock levels, low inventory alerts, and automated restocking recommendations.
- Sales Analytics: Comprehensive dashboards and reports on sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends.
- Data Visualization: Interactive charts and graphs to help you understand your business metrics at a glance.
- Integration: Seamlessly connects with other Uplink modules to provide a unified view of your business operations.

### 🧾 `uplink/ledger`  
Electronic invoicing and financial document management. Issue, track, and archive invoices for compliance and transparency.

This module provides the following features:
- Invoice Generation: Create and customize invoices with support for multiple formats and tax calculations.
- Document Management: Store and organize financial documents securely with easy retrieval and archiving.
- Compliance: Ensure regulatory compliance with features like audit trails, access controls, and data encryption.
- Integration: Connect with payment gateways and accounting software for streamlined financial operations.

### 🏦 `uplink/flow`  
Banking integration made easy. Connect your business accounts to automate payment workflows, reconcile transactions, and streamline financial ops.

This module provides the following features:
- Bank Account Integration: Securely connect multiple bank accounts for real-time transaction monitoring.
- Payment Automation: Set up automated payment workflows for bills, payroll, and vendor payments.
- Transaction Reconciliation: Automatically match transactions with invoices and financial records for accurate bookkeeping.
- Financial Reporting: Generate reports on cash flow, expenses, and financial health with customizable metrics.

### 💵 `uplink/payroll`  
Automated payroll processing. Manage employee compensation, tax calculations, and compliance with local regulations.

This module provides the following features:
- Employee Management: Maintain detailed employee profiles, including compensation, benefits, and tax information.
- Payroll Processing: Automate salary calculations, tax deductions, and direct deposits with support for multiple pay schedules.
- Compliance: Ensure adherence to local labor laws and tax regulations with built-in compliance checks and reporting.
- Integration: Connect with accounting software and banking modules for seamless financial management.

### 💳 `uplink/pos`  
Modern Point-of-Sale system. Designed for physical retail with smooth checkout, item scanning, and sync with e-commerce and inventory.

This module provides the following features:
- Intuitive Checkout: Fast and user-friendly checkout process with support for barcode scanning, discounts, and multiple payment methods.
- Inventory Sync: Real-time synchronization with the inventory management system to ensure accurate stock levels across all sales channels.
- Customer Management: Capture customer information at the point of sale for personalized service and marketing opportunities.
- Reporting: Access sales reports, transaction history, and performance metrics directly from the POS interface.
- Terminal Support: Compatible with a wide range of POS hardware, including tablets, card readers, and receipt printers.


### 📦 `uplink/trace`  
Delivery tracking and logistics orchestration. Monitor order fulfillment, delivery status, and real-time shipment updates.

This module provides the following features:
- Order Fulfillment Tracking: Monitor the status of orders from processing to delivery with real-time updates.
- Logistics Management: Coordinate with multiple carriers and manage shipping options, rates, and delivery times.
- Customer Notifications: Automatically send delivery updates and tracking information to customers via email or SMS.
- Analytics: Analyze delivery performance, identify bottlenecks, and optimize logistics operations with comprehensive reporting.

### 🧭 `uplink/hub`  
The central landing and navigation space. Unified dashboard for accessing and configuring all modules, roles, and user settings is the Back-office of Uplink, providing a centralized interface for managing your entire business ecosystem.

This module provides the following features:
- Centralized Dashboard: A unified interface for accessing all Uplink modules, managing user roles,
and configuring system settings.
- User Management: Create and manage user accounts, assign roles and permissions, and monitor activity logs for enhanced security and accountability.
- System Configuration: Customize settings for each module, including integrations, notifications, and data management preferences.
- Analytics and Reporting: Access high-level insights and reports across all modules to monitor business performance and make informed decisions.
